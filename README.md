EmpowerHer

EmpowerHer is a gamified self-defence learning and safety platform designed to empower women through awareness, skill-building, and confidence. It combines interactive learning modules, progress tracking, and community engagement to help users master self-defence skills in an engaging, accessible, and affordable way.

Overview

Most self-defence platforms today are offline, rigid, and non-interactive, making consistent learning difficult.
EmpowerHer bridges this gap by offering a tech-enabled, gamified solution that encourages women to learn self-defence at their own pace while staying motivated through achievements, levels, and challenges.
